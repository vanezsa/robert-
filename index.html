Dr. Bob’s Tales

Welcome to Dr. Bob’s tales, set in the town of Pondville.

The center of action is a large lively pond. Many diverse residents live here—some in the pond and others in the surrounding hills and trees. For the most part, they all get along well, help one another out, and like to enjoy new adventures.

About the author

Creativity requires inspiration.

Four wonderful brothers and sisters named Sarah, Rachel, Luke and Simon inspired Dr. Bob and helped bring Harry the frog to life.

He began telling them stories about the pond, the surrounding hills and the wildlife that lived there.
